Footnotes

El Paso Corondao's 1983 yearbook states that Robert Pounds placed 3rd in 1982, but does not state the weight class.

Irving MacArthur's 1982 yearbook lists multiple placers without their weight classes: Darrin Upshaw(5th), Mark Bell(5th), Ricky Ward(6th), and Jack Milner(6th).

An article from an El Paso newspaper lists the following placers without their weight classes: Scott O'Brien (3rd, Burges), Jesse Samaniego (3rd, Burges), Mike Pacillas (3rd, Burges), Mike Diaz (3rd, Burges), Jorge Hernandez (3rd, Bowie), Robert Pounds (3rd, Coronado), David Boatwright (4th, Burges), Robert Baca (4th, Eastwood), Jorge Gutierrez (6th, Burges).
