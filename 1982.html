Footnotes

Lake Highlands's 1982 yearbook states the Chris Atlason got 2nd, but does not know the weight class. I'm going to assume his weight class to be 155 since he would go on to wrestle there in 1983, and the yearbook notes that he got second in 1982 to a regional opponent, which could be Ashford of Richardson

El Paso Corondao's 1983 yearbook states that Robert Pounds placed 3rd in 1982, but does not state the weight class.

Irving MacArthur's 1982 yearbook lists multiple placers without their weight classes: Darrin Upshaw(5th), Mark Bell(5th), Ricky Ward(6th), and Jack Milner(6th).

St. Mark's 1982 yearbook states that Craig Copps and Steve Schultz placed 3rd and 6th, respectively, but does not note their weight classes.
