Texas High School State Championships Archive

About

My name is George Youtt, and I am the head boys wrestling coach at Lewisville High School.

I have made this site to archive the history of the Texas State Wrestling from the very beginning. The UIL has archived their tournaments since they started in 1999, but the vast majority of anything before that has existed almost entirely on paper. This archive is not complete, but hopefully I will be able to recover more information as time goes on. I have gotten most of the information on the Pre-UIL tournament from old state programs and coaches' association newsletters. As time goes on, I will be updating the site to include placers, brackets, scans of old programs, and anything else that I can find to preserve the history of Texas High School wrestling.
