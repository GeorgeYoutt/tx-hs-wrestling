Footnotes

Lake Highlands's 1981 yearbook states that Jeff Singleton and Chuck Mina made it to the consolation semifinals, meaning they should have placed, but their places are not noted.

St. Mark's 1981 yearbook notes two state placers, but does not note their weight classes: Nicky Anderson(5th) and David Genecov(6th).

St. Mark's yearbook states that their were 120 teams at the state tournament.
